Chapters of the Society

Six chapters make up the Alpha Delta Phi Society, of which the Brunonian Chapter is a founding member. With the exception of the Stanford Chapter, all Society chapters are within driving distance and Brunonians enjoy roadtripping as well as welcoming visitors from other chapters. In addition, several Fraternity chapters reside in the region.

For perspective on the founding of the Society, please refer to the Brunonian Chapter's History of Co-education or the History of the Alpha Delta Phi Society.

Active Chapters

  • Brunonian Chapter, Brown University, Providence, RI (founded 1836)
  • Columbia Chapter, Columbia University, New York, NY (founded 1836)
  • Middletown Chapter, Wesleyan University, Middletown, CT (founded 1856)
  • Stanford Chapter, Stanford University, Palo Alto, CA (founded 1916)
  • Binghamton Chapter, Binghamton University, Vestal, NY (founded 2011)
  • Capital Chapter, George Washington University, Washington, DC (founded 2014)

Active Affiliates

  • Penn Affiliate, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA (founded 2010)
  • Plattsburgh Affiliate, SUNY Plattsburgh, Plattsburgh, NY (founded 2013)
  • Rensselaer Affiliate, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, NY (founded 2015)
  • Ursinian Affiliate, Ursinus College, Collegeville, PA (founded 2015)

Active Graduate Chapters

  • Bowdoin Chapter, Bowdoin College, Brunswick, ME (founded 1841)
  • Granite Chapter, University of New Hampshire, Durham, NH (founded 2007)

Inactive Chapters

  • Middlebury Chapter, Middlebury College, Middlebury, VT (founded 1994)
